# Privacy Policy for Get It Done

Last updated: March 13, 2026

## Data Collection
Get It Done stores all your tasks locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

## AI Notifications
When you create a task, the task title is sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalized notifications. This data is:
- Only used to create your notification
- Not stored by us
- Not shared with third parties
- Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

## No Tracking
We do not track your usage, analytics, or behavior.

## Local Storage
All todos are stored on your device using AsyncStorage. We cannot access this data.
